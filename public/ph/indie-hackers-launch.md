# Indie Hackers Launch Prep

## Post Title
Show HN: Try AI Writer – AI Writing with Brand Voice Learning & Multiple Models

## Post Body
Hey Indie Hackers! 👋

After 3 months of building, I'm excited to launch Try AI Writer — an AI writing tool that actually learns your brand voice.

**What it does:**
- 🧠 Brand Voice Learning: Upload your writing samples and the AI adapts to your tone
- 🤖 Multiple AI Models: Choose between DeepSeek (fast/affordable) and Claude Sonnet (premium quality)
- 📝 Creative Interview Engine: Turns vague ideas ("I need a blog post") into structured content through an interactive interview

**Why I built it:** As a writer and marketer, I hated how generic AI writing sounded. I wanted something that sounded like *me* — not like every other AI-written thing on the internet.

**Tech Stack:** Next.js 15, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, NextAuth, Vercel, Postgres, OpenAI, Anthropic, DeepSeek APIs.

**Current Stage:** Public beta, 500+ users, ~$0 MRR (just launched waitlist for paid plans).

**Goals:**
- 100 signups from Indie Hackers in first week
- Validate paid plan interest
- Get honest feedback

Would love to hear what you think! Check it out at https://tryaiwriter.com — no sign up required to try!
