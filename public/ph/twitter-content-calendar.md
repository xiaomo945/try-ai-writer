## Best Practices

### Timing
- Peak hours: 9-11am EST, 6-8pm EST
- Launch day: Post at 9am, 11am, 2pm, 6pm EST
- Respond to ALL mentions within 1 hour

### Engagement
- Reply to every comment
- Quote tweet positive feedback
- Engage with replies to your tweets
- Follow back new followers

### Images
- Use consistent brand colors
- Keep text minimal (read in feed)
- Use real screenshots when possible
- Dark mode-friendly visuals

### Hashtags
- Primary: #AI #ProductHunt #Writing
- Secondary: #Startup #ContentCreation #Marketing
- Use 2-3 per tweet, not more

---

## Do's and Don'ts

### DO:
- Be authentic and helpful
- Share real value before asking for anything
- Engage personally with replies
- Share behind-the-scenes content
- Celebrate early users and supporters

### DON'T:
- Hard sell in every tweet
- Auto-DM people who follow you
- Spam the same message repeatedly
- Ignore negative feedback
- Engage with troll comments
