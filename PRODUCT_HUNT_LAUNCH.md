# Use AI Writer Product Hunt Launch Checklist

## Pre-Launch (7 Days Before)

- [ ] Send an email to your subscriber list letting them know you're launching soon
- [ ] Post teasers on social media (Twitter/X, LinkedIn, Reddit)
- [ ] Reach out to friends, colleagues, and past users to ask for support on launch day
- [ ] Prepare your Product Hunt page (name, tagline, description, media, etc.)

## Launch Day

- [ ] Post your product on Product Hunt first thing in the morning (Pacific Time)
- [ ] Share the Product Hunt link on social media
- [ ] Post on relevant Reddit communities (r/ProductHunt, r/SaaS, r/artificial, etc.)
- [ ] Share in relevant Discord/Slack groups
- [ ] Engage with every comment on your Product Hunt post
- [ ] Post updates throughout the day to keep momentum going

## Post-Launch

- [ ] Send a thank-you email to everyone who supported you
- [ ] Analyze the results (traffic, signups, feedback)
- [ ] Iterate on the product based on user feedback
- [ ] Keep the momentum going with regular content and updates
