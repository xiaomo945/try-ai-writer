# AI Writing for SMS Marketing: Drive Results with Every Text

SMS marketing has incredibly high open rates—often 90% or more. But with limited characters (just 160 for a standard SMS), your copy needs to be perfect. AI writing can help you make the most of every character.

## Why SMS Marketing Works

SMS marketing is effective because:
- People check their phones constantly
- Open rates are much higher than email
- Messages are delivered instantly
- It's personal and direct

But it's easy to get wrong and annoy your customers.

## How AI Helps with SMS Marketing

### 1. Write Concise, Impactful Copy

You don't have space for fluff. AI can help you:
- Write tight, clear messages
- Focus on the key message
- Remove unnecessary words
- Make every character count

Try AI Writer to draft, then edit for maximum impact.

### 2. Generate Multiple Variations for Testing

Small changes can make a big difference. Use AI to:
- Create 10+ variations of each SMS
- Test different offers
- Try different CTAs
- Find what resonates with your audience

### 3. Personalize at Scale

Personalized SMS performs better. AI can help you:
- Use customer data to customize messages
- Include names, purchase history, or location
- Segment your audience
- Send relevant, timely offers

### 4. Maintain Brand Voice Consistently

Even short texts should sound like you. AI lets you:
- Train on your brand voice
- Ensure consistent tone across all messages
- Adapt to different campaign types
- Keep your brand personality

## AI Workflows for SMS Marketing

### Workflow 1: Promotion SMS Creation
1. Define your offer
2. Use AI to generate 10 variations
3. Pick the best 3 to test
4. Send to a small segment
5. Roll out the winner to everyone

### Workflow 2: Personalized Campaign Setup
1. Segment your audience
2. Use AI to draft personalized messages
3. Set up triggers (e.g., birthday, abandoned cart)
4. Launch the campaign
5. Track results and optimize

### Workflow 3: SMS Calendar Planning
1. Plan your SMS schedule (don't spam!)
2. Use AI to draft each message
3. Review and refine
4. Schedule them
5. Monitor performance

## Best Practices for SMS Marketing

### Get Permission First
Always make sure people opt-in to receive your texts.

### Provide Value
Every message should be useful to the recipient.

### Include a Clear CTA
Tell people exactly what you want them to do.

### Keep It Short
Stick to 160 characters if possible.
