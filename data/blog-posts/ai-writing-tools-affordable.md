## How to Maximize Value

### Strategy 1: Use Free Tiers Strategically

Many tools offer unlimited free access with limitations. Use this to:

- Generate first drafts (free)
- Edit and refine in a different tool
- Combine multiple AI outputs

### Strategy 2: Pay for What You Need

Don't overpay for features you won't use:

- Solopreneurs: Free or $9/month is plenty
- Small teams: $20-50/month range
- Agencies: Custom pricing

### Strategy 3: Stack Tools

Combine affordable tools for better results:

- Use AI Writer for drafts (free tier)
- Use Hemingway for editing (free)
- Use Grammarly for proofreading ($12/month)

Total cost: Under $15/month for professional-grade content

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

### Mistake #1: Chasing Free Tools Only

Free doesn't always mean valuable. Sometimes paying a small amount saves hours of frustration.

### Mistake #2: Buying Expensive Tools Prematurely

Start with free tiers. Upgrade only when you hit limitations.

### Mistake #3: Relying 100% on AI

AI is a tool, not a replacement for your expertise and voice.
