### 2. Jasper

Jasper is a well-known AI writing platform for marketing teams. It offers templates, team collaboration, and integrations.

- **Free plan**: 7-day trial
- **Paid plans**: From $49/mo
- **Best for**: Enterprise marketing teams
- **Downside**: Expensive for solo creators

### 3. Copy.ai

Copy.ai focuses on short-form copy like ads, emails, and social captions. It's fast and beginner-friendly.

- **Free plan**: 2,000 words/mo
- **Paid plans**: From $36/mo
- **Best for**: Social media and ad copy

### 4. Writesonic

Writesonic offers blog writing, SEO tools, and chatbot features. It's a versatile option for content marketers.

- **Free plan**: Limited credits
- **Paid plans**: From $13/mo
- **Best for**: SEO-optimized blog posts

### 5. Rytr

Rytr is an affordable AI writer with a simple interface. It supports multiple languages and tones.

- **Free plan**: 10,000 characters/mo
- **Paid plans**: From $9/mo
- **Best for**: Budget-conscious writers

### 6. Sudowrite

Sudowrite is designed for fiction writers. It helps with creative storytelling, descriptions, and plot ideas.

- **Free plan**: Trial
- **Paid plans**: From $19/mo
- **Best for**: Novelists and creative writers

### 7. GrammarlyGO

GrammarlyGO adds generative AI to Grammarly's well-known editing platform. It's great for improving existing drafts.

- **Free plan**: Limited AI prompts
- **Paid plans**: From $12/mo
- **Best for**: Editing and rewriting

### 8. Anyword

Anyword uses predictive analytics to help marketers write copy that converts. It's useful for ads and landing pages.

- **Free plan**: 7-day trial
- **Paid plans**: From $49/mo
- **Best for**: Conversion-focused copy

### 9. Hypotenuse AI

Hypotenuse AI focuses on e-commerce content, including product descriptions and blog posts.

- **Free plan**: 7-day trial
- **Paid plans**: From $29/mo
- **Best for**: E-commerce teams

### 10. Writer.com

Writer.com is an enterprise-grade AI writing assistant with strong compliance and team features.

- **Free plan**: Trial
- **Paid plans**: From $18/user/mo
- **Best for**: Large teams with compliance needs
