## Best Practices for Using AI in Social Media

AI is powerful, but it works best with human guidance.

### 1. Keep Your Brand Voice Central

Define your brand voice clearly, then use AI tools that can learn and replicate it. **Use AI Writer’s** brand voice feature is perfect for this.

### 2. Use AI for Ideas, Not Just Finished Posts

AI is great for generating options and variations, but humans should still make the final call.

### 3. Add Human Authenticity

Even the best AI can’t replace genuine human connection. Add personal touches, inside jokes, and real-time responses that only a human can provide.

### 4. Test Everything

One of the biggest advantages of AI is the ability to create multiple variations quickly. Use this to A/B test relentlessly.
