## Integrating AI Into Your Workflow

### The Copywriting Process

**Discovery Phase (You Lead)**
- Client consultation
- Understanding goals and audience
- Research and competitive analysis
- Strategy development

**Drafting Phase (AI Assists)**
- Generate multiple variations
- Create options for testing
- Expand or condense as needed
- Try different angles quickly

**Refinement Phase (You Lead)**
- Select best options
- Inject creative spark
- Ensure brand voice
- Quality control

**Delivery Phase (Collaboration)**
- Client review
- Revisions
- Final polish

### When to Use AI (And When Not To)

**Use AI for:**
- First drafts and variations
- Research summaries
- Structural frameworks
- Overcoming blank-page syndrome
- Bulk content production

**Don't Use AI for:**
- Client strategy sessions
- Final creative direction
- Relationship building
- Complex brand voice development
- Sensitive or high-stakes copy

## AI Prompts for Common Copywriting Projects

### Website Copy

**Homepage:**
> "Write homepage copy for [company]. They serve [audience] who want [outcome]. Their unique value: [UVP]. Include: headline, subheadline, 3 benefit sections, social proof placeholder, and CTA. Tone: [brand voice]."

**About Page:**
> "Write an about page for [company/founder]. Include: origin story, mission, values, team culture, and what makes them different. Tone: [conversational/professional/friendly]."

**Product Description:**
> "Write product descriptions for [product line]. Focus on: benefits over features, emotional triggers, and clear value proposition. Target audience: [description]. Include: headline, key benefits (3-5), and CTA."

### Email Marketing

**Welcome Sequence:**
> "Write a 5-email welcome sequence for [company]. Goal: onboard new subscribers and introduce [product/service]. Each email should: have compelling subject line, serve specific purpose, include value, and have clear CTA."

**Promotional Email:**
> "Write a promotional email for [offer]. Include: attention-grabbing subject line, opening hook, problem agitate solution structure, offer details, urgency elements, and CTA button text. Tone: exciting but not sleazy."

### Ad Copy

**Google Ads:**
> "Write 10 Google Ad variations for [product/service]. Include: 3 headlines (30 char limit) and 2 descriptions (90 char limit) per ad. Focus on: [primary benefit], [secondary benefit], [urgency]."

**Facebook/Instagram Ads:**
> "Write 5 Facebook ad copy variations for [offer]. Each should have: different hook angle, body copy under 125 words, and CTA. Test these angles: [list angles]."

### Social Media

**LinkedIn Posts:**
> "Write 10 LinkedIn post templates for [industry/niche]. Include: hook, value content, and CTA. Vary: educational, personal story, controversial take, how-to, and list format."

**Instagram Captions:**
> "Write 15 Instagram caption variations for [brand]. Each should: have engaging hook, deliver value, use [tone], and include CTA. Include relevant hashtags."

## Building a Template Library
