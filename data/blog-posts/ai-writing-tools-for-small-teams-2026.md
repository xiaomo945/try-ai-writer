# Best AI Writing Tools for Small Teams in 2026

Small teams need AI writing tools that are affordable, collaborative, and easy to use. Here are our top picks for 2026.

## Key Features Small Teams Should Look For

When evaluating AI writing tools for your team, prioritize these features:

### 1. Brand Voice Training
Consistency is everything. Look for tools that let you train the AI on your team's existing content so everyone writes in the same voice.

### 2. Collaboration & Version History
Team members need to work together. Version history lets you see changes and revert if needed.

### 3. Shared Memory & Context
No need to start over every time. Tools that remember your team's past work save time and keep content consistent.

### 4. Affordable Pricing
Small teams don't have enterprise budgets. Look for tools with transparent pricing and no hidden fees.

## How to Choose the Right Tool for Your Team

1. **Start with your goals**: Are you writing blog posts, emails, social media?
2. **Test the brand voice feature**: Does it understand your team's style?
3. **Check collaboration features**: Can multiple people work together easily?
4. **Evaluate pricing**: Is it affordable for your team size?

## Tips for Getting Started

- **Start small**: Train the AI on 5-10 of your best pieces first
- **Set guidelines**: Create a simple style guide for your team
