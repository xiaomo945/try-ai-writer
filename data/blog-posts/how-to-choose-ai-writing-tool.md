# How to Choose the Right AI Writing Tool: A 7-Point Checklist

With so many AI writing tools available, choosing the right one can feel overwhelming. This checklist will help you evaluate options systematically.

## The 7-Point Evaluation Framework

### 1. Voice Consistency

**Why it matters**: Your brand voice is your identity.

**What to check**:
- Can it learn from your existing content?
- Does it maintain consistency across generations?
- Can you customize voice profiles?

### 2. Output Quality

**Why it matters**: Bad output wastes your time.

**What to check**:
- Sample outputs before committing
- User reviews mentioning quality
- Tone and accuracy of generated text

### 3. Use Case Coverage

**Why it matters**: You need one tool for all content types.

**What to check**:
- Blog posts
- Emails
- Social media
- Long-form content
- Technical documentation

### 4. Integration Capabilities

**Why it matters**: Your workflow matters.

**What to check**:
- API availability
- CMS integrations
- Browser extensions
- Mobile access

### 5. Pricing Structure

**Why it matters**: Budget matters, especially for startups.

**What to check**:
- Free tier availability
- Cost per generation
- Unlimited plans
- Enterprise pricing

Try AI Writer offers transparent pricing with a generous free tier, Pro at $9/month, and Max at $25/month.

### 6. Learning Curve

**Why it matters**: Time is money.

**What to check**:
- Setup complexity
- Time to first quality output
- Quality of documentation
- Customer support availability

### 7. Privacy and Security

**Why it matters**: Your data is valuable.

**What to check**:
- Data retention policies
- Training data usage
- GDPR compliance
- API security

## Red Flags to Watch For

- Vague pricing with "contact sales" for basic info
- No free trial or money-back guarantee
- Poor customer reviews mentioning quality issues
- Unclear data policies
