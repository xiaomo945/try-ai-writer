Referral marketing leverages existing customer relationships to acquire new ones. Referred customers often have higher lifetime value and better retention rates than customers acquired through advertising. Yet referral requests must feel generous—inviting customers to share value with friends—rather than demanding favors.

AI writing tools help businesses create referral request campaigns that transform satisfied customers into active advocates.

## Referral Psychology

### Why Referrals Work

People trust recommendations from friends and family more than any advertising. Referrals carry implicit endorsement that marketing cannot replicate.

### The Psychology of Asking

Asking for referrals activates reciprocity—customers who've received value want to share that value with others. Effective referral requests frame advocacy as helping friends.

Referral request subject lines must feel generous rather than demanding. AI helps you craft subject lines that communicate mutual benefit.
